About the Initiative

Vempalli Child Hope is a community-led awareness program for children in Vempalli Mandal, Kadapa District. We spread accurate information, connect families to support, and provide a safe channel to report suspected child labour.

  • Awareness camps in schools and villages
  • Guidance on government schemes & helplines
  • Confidential reporting to the Mandal Education Officer (MEO)

Know the Law (India)

Child & Adolescent Labour (Prohibition & Regulation) Act, 1986/2016

Prohibits employment of children below 14 years in all occupations and adolescents (14–18) in hazardous occupations.

RTE Act, 2009

Makes education free and compulsory for children aged 6–14. Every child belongs in school, not at work.

JJ Act, 2015

Provides care and protection for children in need, including those rescued from labour.

How to Act

Call 1098 (Childline), inform local authorities, or use the report form below. Your identity can be kept confidential.

Spot the Signs

Missing school or regular absence

Children frequently working miss classes or drop out entirely. Report if you notice consistent absence.

Working long hours / hazardous tasks

Handling heavy machinery, chemicals, night shifts, or excessive hours is unsafe and illegal for children.

Visible stress, fatigue, or injuries

Unexplained injuries, exhaustion, or fear of employers are red flags requiring attention.
